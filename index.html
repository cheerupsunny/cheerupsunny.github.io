Welcome to Yuwei's Foodie Corner!

Mission Statement

"Yuwei’s Foodie Corner" is a website that offers a brief introduction about Yuwei and a recording of one of her hobbies: cooking and finding yummy restaurants.

Hopefully, this website can help people relax and get fun from cooking even in a fast-speed era, as well as provide inspiration to people about what to have for meals in Canberra.

Unlike most websites that contain intense information nowadays, this website aims to offer a chill and cosy atmosphere with a user-friendly and easy-to-access interface and a minimalist design style.

Goal List

  • Get to know Yuwei (Hi, nice to meet you!)
  • Get to know some of Yuwei’s favourite self-made dishes
  • Get to know Yuwei’s list of "yummy restaurants in Canberra"
  • Get information about some representative regional cuisines
