Original Recipe

Explore my secret recipe of Sichuan boiled pork with me!

Tip: click the dots below the pictures to switch pages

Ingredients

1. Pork fillet
2. Some veggies such as tofu and mushrooms
3. Spices: Ginger, Chinise five spice, Soy sauce, Cook wine, Potato starch, Spring onion, Garlic, Chilli powder, Sichun pepper powder
4. Spicy hotpot broth

Preparation

1. Thinly slice the pork fillet
2. Chop the spring onion and garlic
2. Add ginger, chinese five spice, soy sauce, cook wine, potato starch and part of the chopped spring onion to the sliced pork
3. Mix well and marinate the pork for 20 minutes
4. Put the rest of the chopped spring onion, chopped garlic, chilli powder and Sichuan pepper powder together into a small bowl

Cooking

1. Put the spicy hotpot broth to a pot with boiling water
2. Put the viggies you like in and boil for 5 minites
3. Put the marinated pork in, stir and boil for 1-2 minutes
4. Use another pan to heat up some oil
5. Put the mixture in preparation 4 on top of the contents in step 2 and pour the heated oil in
