About My Site

Check my reflections on developing this website here!


User-Centred Design

It is crucial to address user-centred design in website development. Without understanding user needs, a website is meaningless despite how useful its contents are. Also, users may prefer different types of communication, thus it is better to present different forms of information (E.g.,text, audio, visual etc.) on the website.

Although my website has no specific target users at this stage, I still value feedback from any potential user. By presenting my website to my friends, family and tutors, I gained valuable suggestions and improved my design correspondingly.

Design Consistency

Design consistency plays an important role in the final layout of the website and thus affects user experience directly. If a website can achieve consistency in its design elements such as colours, fonts, text styles, and layout patterns across all pages, it ensures users' visual experience by offering familarity and predicatbility.

Thus, I used harmonious but also distinguishable colour schemes, consistent font hierarchy and a general CSS file for parts with similar designs, aiming to offer a user-friendly overall design and enhance user experience.

Keep Updated

Website contents can change frequently during the process of development, including introducing new concepts, modifying previous designs and so on, thus it is crucial to keep the website updated constantly. Failing to do so may result in website inconsistency and confusing user experience.

In the context of my experience, when I added new pages with the expansion of my website, I kept updating the related sections correspondingly, such as the site map, source log and on-top navigating bar.
